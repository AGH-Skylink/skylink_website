What we do?

  • satellite_alt

    Space and Laser Communication

    We design and test advanced satellite and free-space optical communication systems — from dual-band ground terminals to high-speed laser links enabling reliable data transfer in extreme conditions.

  • electric_bolt

    Microelectronics

    Our team develops custom electronic circuits, embedded software, and RF systems that power our communication, control, and measurement solutions for space and scientific missions.

  • radar

    Radar

    We build innovative radar systems, including a drone-mounted ground-penetrating radar tested in the Himalayas, capable of subsurface imaging and exploration in challenging environments.
